The Clicking Dead!

We know just how trying living in a walker filled world can be, so we created this quick guide to help you survive the walker apocalypse.

First and foremost, there are two meters at the top of the screen. Make sure to monitor these carefully!

  • The top bar indicates the danger of being overrun by walkers.
  • The second bar indicates the amount of supplies left in your location.

I'm sure you're wondering, how do I manage the risk and find supplies. It's quite simple:

  • Click "Kill Walkers" to kill walkers and reduce your risk of being overrun.
  • Click "Scavenge" to find useful supplies in the environment.

To make the game more interesting, there is also the probability of random events occuring. Your weapons, upgrades, armor, and companions also affect the gameplay. For example, having lots of loud, noisy weapons will attract more walkers.

Good luck!
