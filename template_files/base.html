Welcome to
The Clicking Dead!

We know just how trying living in a zombie filled world can be, so we created this quick guide to help you survive the zombie apocalypse.

First and foremost, there are two meters at the top of the screen. Make sure to monitor these carefully!

  • The top bar indicates the danger of being overrun by zombies.
  • The second bar indicates the amount of supplies left in your location.

I'm sure you're wondering, how do I manage the risk and find supplies. Its quite simple:

  • Click "Kill Zombies" to kill zombies and reduce your risk of being overrun.
  • Click "Scavenge" to find useful supplies in the environment.

To make the game more interesting, there is also the probability of random events occuring. Your weapons, upgrades, armor, and companions also affect the gameplay. For example, having lots of loud noisy weapons will attract more zombies.

Good luck!
