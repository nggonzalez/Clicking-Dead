The Clicking Dead!

We know just how difficult living in a walker filled world can be, so we created this quick guide to help you survive the walker apocalypse.

First and foremost, there are two meters at the top of the screen. Make sure to monitor these carefully!

  • The top bar indicates the danger of being overrun by walkers. If you are overrun, you lose some supplies
  • The second bar indicates the amount of supplies left in your location. If this gets to zero, you scavenge much less efficiently.

I'm sure you're wondering, how do I manage the risk and find supplies. It's quite simple:

  • Click "Kill Walkers" to kill walkers and reduce your risk of being overrun.
  • Click "Scavenge" to find useful supplies in the environment.
  • Buy "Companions" and "Weapons" to help you find supplies and kill walkers.

To make the game more interesting, there is also the probability of random events occuring. Your weapons, upgrades, and companions affect the gameplay.

Good luck!
